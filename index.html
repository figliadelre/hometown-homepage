Collingswood

New Jersey

Historic sites, upscale eats, and community vibes in the heart of South Jersey.

PLAN YOUR VISIT. WE KNOW YOU'LL LOVE IT HERE.

Historic Downtown

Stroll the shops, stop for coffee, or dine in one of over 40 eateries! And don't forget to bring your own bottle... just one of the perks of dining in Collingswood.

Knight Park

Take an evening stroll through the park. Enjoy 4 acres of natural beauty nestled just off Haddon Avenue. Attend a sporting event, playground, or bring the pup to a daily dog meet.

Clover Market

April thru October you can visit our open air farmer's market every Saturday, rain or shine. Enjoy local produce, baked goods, and locally crafted items. Live music featured every week!

Meet Your Local Guide!
